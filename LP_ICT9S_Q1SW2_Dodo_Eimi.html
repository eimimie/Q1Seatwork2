CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE

Crisostomo Ibarra
He is the main character of the story and he studied in Spain for 7 years.
Maria Clara
She is the daughter of Kapitan Tiago and Donya Pia Alba, but is the real daughter of Padre Damaso.
Donya Pia Alba
She is the mother of Maria Clara and the wife of Kapitan Tiago.
